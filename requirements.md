Zchuchit

A windoes app that is designed for visually impaired people, that needs tools to see better when working in their PC.

Features:
1. Magnifying glass, that can be triggered with a shortcut, that popups on their screen, and magnigifies the selected area in their mouse
2. The magnified area can be of the following shapes:
    - Rectangle
    - Circle
3. The magnified area can be resized with a bar on top of the screen
